Section 04

Revenue Model

Simple mechanics. Transparent structure. Every euro tracked.

1
Guest Discovers

Guest checks into Czech Inn Hotel. Sees QR card on the desk. Scans it. Lands on Czech Inn Hotels Experiences — branded platform with curated tours, Gold Label options, and city extras.

2
Guest Books

Selects a tour. Chooses date, language, group size. Pays securely via Stripe. Receives instant confirmation with meeting point, guide name, and WhatsApp contact. Takes 90 seconds.

3
Hotel Earns

25% commission auto-calculated on every booking. Aggregated by property. Czech Inn Hotels receives a monthly payout report and bank transfer. Zero administration required.
