QR-Access, No App Required

Guests scan a QR code on their room key card or table topper. The full experience hub opens instantly in their browser. No app download, no account creation, no friction.

Multilingual Support

English, Czech, German, French, and Spanish interfaces. Auto-detected from browser language. Your international guests feel at home from the moment they arrive.

Live Booking and Availability

Real-time tour availability, instant confirmation, and WhatsApp or email ticket delivery. No calling, no waiting, no friction.

AI Recommendation Engine

Based on check-in details including travel party, interests, weather, and time of day, the concierge surfaces the three most relevant experiences. Conversion rates 3 times higher than static menus.

Guest Memory Book

After each tour, guides upload photos to a private guest album. Guests receive a beautiful digital memory book, and a gentle review request. Average review rate: 68%.

Smart Pre-Arrival Emails

48 hours before check-in, guests receive a personalised email with weather forecast, top 3 tour recommendations, and a direct booking link. Conversion on these emails: 22%.
