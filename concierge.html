Platform Features

Everything a Guest
Could Wish For

QR-Access, No App Required

Guests scan a QR code on their room key card or table topper — the full experience hub opens instantly in their browser. No app download, no account creation, no friction.

Multilingual Support

English, Czech, German, French, and Spanish interfaces. Auto-detected from browser language. Your international guests feel at home from the moment they arrive.

Live Booking & Availability

Real-time tour availability, instant confirmation, and WhatsApp or email ticket delivery. No calling, no waiting, no "check with reception."

AI Recommendation Engine

Based on check-in details — travel party, interests, weather, and time of day — the concierge surfaces the three most relevant experiences. Conversion rates 3× higher than static menus.

Guest Memory Book

After each tour, guides upload photos to a private guest album. Guests receive a beautiful digital "memory book" — and a gentle review request. Average review rate: 68%.

Smart Pre-Arrival Emails

48 hours before check-in, guests receive a personalised email — weather forecast, top 3 tour recommendations, and a direct booking link. Conversion on these emails: 22%.
